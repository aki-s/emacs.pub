channel-del - remove a channel

Introduction

Remote a channel from the registry. Note that the default channels cannot be removed from the registry.

Default channels in Pyrus:

  • __uri
  • pear.php.net
  • pear2.php.net
  • pecl.php.net
  • doc.php.net
